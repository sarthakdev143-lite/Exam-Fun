Section A: Tenses

Q1. Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in parentheses (5 × 2 = 10 marks):

  1. She __________ (go) to school every day.
  2. They __________ (finish) their homework before dinner last night.
  3. By next week, he __________ (complete) the project.
  4. We __________ (play) cricket when it started raining.
  5. The train __________ (arrive) at the station by 9 PM.

Q2. Rewrite the following sentences by changing the tense as indicated (4 × 2 = 8 marks):

  1. She writes a letter. (Change to past continuous)
  2. They had finished their work. (Change to future perfect)
  3. He is reading a book. (Change to present perfect)
  4. We will attend the meeting. (Change to past simple)

Section D: Transformation of Sentences

Q4. Transformation 1 (Change the sentences as directed) (4 × 2 = 8 marks):

  1. "I have never seen such a beautiful painting." (Change to: "Never before ...")
  2. "She is so talented that she won the competition." (Change to start with "Such ...")
  3. "The students completed the assignment on time." (Change to passive voice)
  4. "He said, 'I will visit you tomorrow.'" (Change to reported speech)
